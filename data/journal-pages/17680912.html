Monday 12 NNW S 40° W 102 32° 43' 15° 53' -------------------------------

Moderate breeze and fine clear weather. At 6 am the Island of Porto Santo bore NWBW, Distce 9 or 10 Leagues. Hauld the wind to the westward at noon, the Desertas extending from WSW. to SWBS, the Body of Madeira W1/2S and Porto Santo NNW1/2W. Latd obser'd 32° 43' N.
