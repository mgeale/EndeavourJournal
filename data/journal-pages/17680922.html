Week Days Month Days Winds Courses Distce in Miles Latitude in Longd in West from Greenwich Bearings at Noon
Thursday 22 SW:erly S 35° 30' E 73 29° 40' 15° 31' Do N 21° W Dt 62 Leags.

Gentle breeze and clear weather. At 4 pm saw the Salvages bearing South, at 6 the Body of the Island bore S1/2W Distt about 5 leagues. Found the Variation of the Compass by an Azimuth to be 17° 50' West. At 10 the Isles of Salvages bore WBS1/2S distt 2 leagues. I make those Islands to lie in Latd 30° 11' S and S 16° E, 58 Leagues from Fonchall Madeira.
