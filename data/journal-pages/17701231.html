Monday 31 At 1 PM the wind veer'd to the northward    we Tackd and stood to the westward and weatherd Pulo Baby.   In the Evening Anchord between this Island and Bantam Bay, the Island bearing North distant 2 Miles and Bantam point West. At 5 AM wieghd with the wind at WBS which afterwards proved Varble. At Noon Bantam point SW1/2W distant 3 Leagues — .
