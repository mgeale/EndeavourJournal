Monday 2d In the PM had a Moderate gale at North with thick hazy weather attended with rain - At 8 oClock it fell little wind and veer'd to WSW at which time we tack'd. At Midnight the wind came to SSW and increased to a brisk gale with fair Clowdy weather which we made the most of as soon as it was day light - At Noon our Latitude by observation was 40°.0' and Longde made from Cape Farewell 2°..31' West —
