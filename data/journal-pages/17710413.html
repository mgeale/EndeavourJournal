Saturday 13th Fresh breezes at SW and Clowdy hazey weather- In the night Anchord here a Dutch Ship from Holland, she saild about 3 Months ago in company with two more, the news brought by this Ship is that a War was Dayly expected between England and Spain — Signals out for 4 or 5 sail more being in the offing one of which is said to be a Ship from England - Took leave of the Governor intending to Sail to morrow —
