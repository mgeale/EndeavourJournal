Tuesday 23rd In the PM hauld along side one of the Warfes, in order to take out our stores &Ca, after which the Ship is to be deliverd into the Charge of the proper officers at Onrust, who will / as I am inform'd / heave her down and repair her with their own people ^only while ours must stand and look on, who if we were permited could do every thing wanting to the Ship our selves —
