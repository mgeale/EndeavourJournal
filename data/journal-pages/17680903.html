Week Days Month Days Winds Course Distce Latd in Longd in West from Greenwich Bearings at Noon
Saturday 3 SW & West S 68° 45' 44 44° 9' 10° 20' Lizd N 291/2° E Dist. 138 leagues

First part Little wind and hazey with rain, remainder Strong gales with hard squalls which brought us under our close reefed top sails and obliged us to strike topgallant yards. At 8 AM wore ship and stood to the Southward.
