Wednesday 19th This morning Lycurgus, whose real name is Tobia Tomita Toobouratomita came with his Family from the westward in order from what we could understand to live near us, he brought with him the covering of a house with several other Materials for building one, we intend to requite the conf[i]dence this man seems to put in us by treating him with all imaginable kindness- got on Shore some empty casks which we place'd in a double row along the bank of the River by way of a breast work on that side —
