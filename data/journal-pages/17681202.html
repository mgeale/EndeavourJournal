Friday 2nd This morning sent a Packet for the Secretary of the Admiralty, on board the Spanish Packet Hopp Don Antonio Monte Negro Velasco, commander - contain^ing copies of all the Memorials and letters that hath have pass'd between the Viceroy and me, and likewise a nother Packet containing duplicates thereof I left with the Viceroy to be by him forwarded to Lisbon. At 9 weigh'd and came to sail and turned down the Bay - Peter Flower seaman fell over board and before any assistance could be given him was drownd in his room we got a Portuguse —   [3]
