Sunday 30th Fresh gales and fair weather. In the AM I took into my posission the Officers, Petty officers and Seamens Log Books & Journals, at least all that I could find and enjoyn'd every one not to divulge where they had been. At Noon our Course and distance saild sence yesterday Noon is No 20° West 126 Miles, which brought us into the Latitude of 7°..34' and Longitude 255°..13' Wt
