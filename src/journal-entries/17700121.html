Sunday 21st PM a gentle breeze of wind Southerly the remainder light airs and calms, with clear settled weather. PM the People employed as usual and at 8 oClock we return'd on board with Pinnace from surveying the Bay in the doing of which I met with an excellent harbour but saw no Inhabitants or any Cultivated land —

In the AM after hauling the Saine for fish I gave every body leave to go a Shore at the Watering place to amuse themselves as they thought proper —
