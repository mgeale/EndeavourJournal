Saturday, 29th This day got the four Guns out of the Hold and mounted 2 of them on the Quarter Deck.  and the other 2 in the Fort on the bank of the River —

For this day or 2 past about 30 Double Canoes, in which might be between 2 & 300 people, had come into our neighbourhood   this made us keep a very good lookout & a strick eye over all their motions —
