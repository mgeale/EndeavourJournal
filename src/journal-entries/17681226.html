Monday 26 North SW 158 40°..19' 54° 30'  

A Fresh breeze of Wind and Clowdy weather; pass'd by some rockweed. At noon the Observed Latitude 26 Miles to the Southward of the Logg - which I believe is chiefly owing to her being generally steerd to the Southward of her Course, yesterday being Christmas day the people were none of the Soberest
