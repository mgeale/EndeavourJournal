Wednesday 9th Winds Northerly most part a fresh breeze with which we stood off ^shore untill 12 at night, at the distance of 5 Leagues from the land had 70 fathom, at the distance of 6 Leagues 80 fathom which is the extent of the Soundings for at the distance of 10 Leagues off we had no ground with 150 fathom - Stood in shore untill 8 oClock AM and hardly fetched the C. Three Points - Having little wind at NWBN we tack'd and stood off untill Noon at which time we tackd with the wind at NNE, being than in the Latde of 33°..37' So Cape Three Points bearing NWBW distant four Leagues —
