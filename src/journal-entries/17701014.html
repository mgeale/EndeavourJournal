Sunday 14th Early in the Morning a ship saild from hence for Holland by which I had just time to write two or three lines to Mr Stephens Secretary of the Admiralty to acquaint him of our Arrival after which I went a shore and waited upon the Shap bander Shabander which is who has the derection of the Town, port &Ca — in order to get an order to the Superintendant at Unrust to Receive us at that Island, but this I was told would not be ready before Tuesday next — Received from the Shore fresh beef and greens for the Ships Compney —
