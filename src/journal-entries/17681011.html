Tuesday 11 SEerly S 52° W 18 7° 36' 22° 8' Do N 3° E Dt 168 Leags

Weather very variable with frequent squalls, rain and Lightning. By the observed Latd at Noon I find the ship hath only made 22 Miles Southing since the last observation two days ago, whereas the Logg gives 55 Miles -- a prooff that there is a Current seting to the Northward.
