First part Calm, Middle light breezes, latter fresh breeze and hazey    PM had several azimuths all of which gave the Variation less then 4° East, but they were a little doubtfull on account of the rowling of the Ship; what winds we have had this Day hath been from the Eastward and are the first we have had from that quarter sence we left the Latd 58° 46' —
