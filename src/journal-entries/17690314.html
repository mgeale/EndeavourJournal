Week Days Month Days Winds Course's Distce Sail'd in Miles Latd In South Longd in Wt from Greenwich Bearings at Noon
Tuesday 14 South ESE ENE No 50° W. 47. 30°..17' 126..10  

Little wind and fine pleasant weather; at 3 PM took several Observations of the Sun and Moon.   the mean result of which gave 126°..20'..45" the Longitude of the Ship west of Greenwich, and is 47' of Longitude west of Account carried on from Cape Horn.
