Friday 16 Easterly ----- ----- ----- ----- -----

The most part fine clear weather. Punished Henry Stevens Seaman, and Tho.s Dunister Marine with 12 lashes each for refusing to take their allowance of fresh Beef. Empd taking on board wine and water.
