First part Strong gales with excessive hard Squales accompney'd with rain, at 9 PM wore and brought too her head to the westward under the Main sail, and reef'd the Fore sail for the first time — the Storm continued with little intermission untill a little toward Noon when it abated so as we could set the Topsails close reef'd — Saw ma[n]y Penguins and some Seals —
