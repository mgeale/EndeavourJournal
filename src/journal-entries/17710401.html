Monday 1st Apl In the PM I observ'd a dark dence haze like a fog bank in the S.E. Horizon and white clowds began to gather over the Table Mountain, certain signs of an aproaching gale from the same quarter, which about 4 oClock began to blow with great Voylance and continued more or less so the remainder of these 24 hours, the Table mountain Cap'd with white Clowds all the time, the weather dry and clear
