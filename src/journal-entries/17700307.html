Wednesday 7th Light airs in the SW quarter    PM clear weather the remainder dark and Clowdy. In the PM found the Variation per several Azths and the Ampde to 15°..10' Et and by the Ampde in the morning to be 15°..56' East. Stood to the SE untill 8 AM then tack'd and stood to the NW.   but it soon after fell calm and continued so untill noon when by our account we were in the Latitude of 47°..6' South and had made 12 Miles Easting sence yesterday at Noon —
