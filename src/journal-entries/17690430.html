Sunday, 30th This being the Day that Owhaa told us that ^ we should fire our Guns, no one of us went from the Fort ^ except such as were sent out to watch the Motions of the natives   however the day past over without any Visable alteration in the beheavour of any one of the Natives them   Queen Obaria visited us this morning pretty early and made me sencible that I must give her a hatchet & then she would give me a Pig. I agree'd to her plan and the Pig was produced —
