Saturday 28th Gentle breezes southerly and fine weather. Employ'd Wooding, cuting, of Broom stuff and maing of Brooms, there being a shrub here very fit for that purpose: and as I intendd to sail tomorrow in the morning some hands were employ'd picking of Sellery to take to sea with us, this is found here in great plenty and I have caused it to be boild with Portable Soup and Oatmeal every morning for the Peoples breakfast, and this I design to continue as long as it will last or any is to be got — because I look upon it to be very wholesome and a great Antiscorbutick.
