Saturday 22nd. Winds at SSE, SE and East a gentle breeze which we steer'd WSW by Compass. At 4 oClock we discover'd a small low Island bearing SSW distant 3 Leagues, this Island hath no place in any of our charts ^/ Latde 10°.47' Longd 238°.28' West./. At Noon we were in the Latitude of 101°..9' So Longitude 238°..56' West Course and distance saild sence Yesterday Noon St 63° West 67 miles —
