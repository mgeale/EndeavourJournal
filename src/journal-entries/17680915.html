Thursday 15 NE to SE ----- ----- ----- ----- ------

Squalls of Wind from the land with rain the most part of these 24 hours. Rec'd on board fresh Beef and Greens for the Ship's Compney, and sent on shore all our Casks for wine and Water, having a shoarre boat employ'd for that purpose.
