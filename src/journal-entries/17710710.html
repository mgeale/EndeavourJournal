Wednesday 10 Westerly N. 44° E 97 49..29 6..18 —  

Pleasent breezes & Clear weather. At 6 o'clock in the Morning Sounded and Struck ground in 60 fathom, Shells & Stones by which I judged we were the Length of Scilly Isles — At Noon we saw Land from the Mast head bearing North which we judged to be about the Lands end — Soundings 54 fm Course grey sand —
