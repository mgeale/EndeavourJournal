Saturday 21st Winds from S.S.W to S.W. moderate breezes the first part latter, fresh gales with showers of rain, PM hoisted in the Boats and made ready for sailing and 2 AM weighed and made sail out of the Bay-   at 1/2 past 4 the Cape of Good Success bore Wt and Cape Bartholomew East, Variation pr Azth 24°.9' East —  at Noon the Cape of Good Success bore N. 36° West Distt 11 Leags
