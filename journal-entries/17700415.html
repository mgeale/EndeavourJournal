Sunday 15th In the PM had light airs at North which in the AM increased to a fresh gale with which ^we made the best of our way to the westward and by noon had run sence yesterday upon a S 86°..15' West Course 79 Miles. Latde in by Observation 39°..30' So and Longitude made from Cape Fare-well 20°..2' West — Some flying fish seen to day —
