June 1771       Miles °   .. ' °    ..     '  
Thurs - 6 ENE to E N3/4W 90 26..1 43° 18' pr Obn &  

Weather as yesterday. In ye AM found the Variation by the Mean of ye Ampd & Azths to be 5°..34' West, and by Observation of the Sun and Moon found the Ship in Longd 43°..18' Wt of Greenwich, 2°..51' West of the Log sence the last observations, this I judge to be owing to a westerly Current.
