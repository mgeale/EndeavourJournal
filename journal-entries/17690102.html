The first part of this day a Gentle gale and clear weather, the Middle Squally with Lightning and rain. and some showers of large hail stones, to wards Noon a Steady fresh breeze and clear weather - at Noon Longitude in by 3 sets of observations between the Sun and Moon 61°..7'.. 45" Wt which is 43" to the Eastward of yesterday's observations - the Ship by the Log has made 4' East —
