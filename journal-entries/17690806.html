Sunday 6th Variable light airs and fair weather. AM. I sent the Master again to the northward to procure refreshments, who return'd not unsuccessfull   Opoony the Chief sent some of his people this morning to me for ^to get something in return for the present he sent the other day.   he not chewsing as I suppose to trust himself on board or perhaps he thought the persons he sent / who were three very pretty young girls / would succeed better than he should do.   be this as it may they went away very well saitisfied with what they got.  altho I beleive that they were disapointed in some things —
