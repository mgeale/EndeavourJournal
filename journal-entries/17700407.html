Saturday 7th Gentle breezes at NE which in the AM Veer'd to NW. In the PM found the Variation by the mean of several Azths to be 13°..50' East being than in the Latitude of 37°..23' So and Longitude 196°..44' West   In the AM Punish'd Jno Bowles Marine with twelve lashes for refusing to do what was his duty when orderd both by the Boatswains mate and Sergt of Marines   At Noon Latde pr Obsn 37°.35' So Longde made from Cape Fare-well 11°..34' Wt Course and distance run sence yesterday noon So 70°.15' Wt 50 Miles —
