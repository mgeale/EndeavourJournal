Week Day's &Ca Winds Course's Distce Saild Latd In South Longd in West [of Greenwich] Bearings &Ca at Noon
Mon 21 NNW St 5° Et 80 M. 29..44 148°..22  

Fresh gales & Clowdy Hazey weather. Saw 2 Pintado Birds the first I have seen this Voyage, they are larger than a Pigeon and chequer'd black and white over their backs & wings, with white bellies, black heads, and the ends of their tails are black —
