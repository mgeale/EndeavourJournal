A Fresh Trade and mostly fair weather. Departed this Life Danl Roberts Gunners Servant who died of the flux. sence we have had a fresh Trade wind this fatall disorder hath seem'd to be at a stand, yet there are several people which are so far gone and brought so very low by it that we have not the least hopes of their recovery —
