Tuesday 27 NE S 19° W 145 21 . 26 20 14' Do N. 26° E Dt 154 legs

The same wind and weather this day as yesterday. Served Wine to the Ships Compney, the Beer being all expended to two cask which I intend to keep some time longer, as the whole has proved very good to the Very last Cask. At Noon found the ship by Observation 10 miles a head of the Log, which I suppose may be owing to a current Setting in the same diriction of as the trade wind.
