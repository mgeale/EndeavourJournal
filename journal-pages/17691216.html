Saturday 16th Fresh breeze between the SBW and S.W, Clear weather with a swell from the westward —

At 6 AM Saw the land from the Mast Head bearing S.S.W. got topgt yards up and set the sails; unbent the foresail to repair and brought a nother to the yard —

At Noon Latd Obd 33°..43' So Course made sence yesterday Noon N 60° West distant 56 Miles. The land in sight bearing SBW distant 14 Leagues —
