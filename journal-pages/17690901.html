Week Days &Ca Winds Courses Distce Saild in Miles Latd In South Longd in West [of Greenwich] Bearings &Ca at Noon
September Frid. 1 Westerly S. 29° E 50 40..12 146°..29'  

Very strong gales, and heavy squalls with rain. At 6 P.M. brot too under the Main sail; at 6 AM set the fore sail. A great sea from the Southwa Westward, the same sort of Birds about the Ship as yesterday. but not in such great numbers —
