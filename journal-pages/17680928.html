Wednesday 28 NE ENE S 12° 30' W 150 18° 59' 20° 48' W Peek of Teneriff N. 23° 15' E Dt 204 L.

A Fresh trade wind and hazey weather. The Variation of the Compass by the mean of several Azimuths taken this evening 12° 46' and in the morning by the same Method 12° 43' W. This day Log and observed Latd agree which is not reconsilable to yesterday. Exercised the People at small Arms.
