Monday 19 ESE ----- ----- 31° 43' ----- High Land over Funchall N 7° E Distce 49 miles

Light breeze and fine clear weather. At Midnight Saild from Funchal, at 8 am the high land over it bore N½E. Unbent the cables stowed the Anchors and Issued to the Ship's Compney 10 pounds of Onions p.r Man. Ship's Draught of Water, Forw:rd 14F. 8I. Abaft 15F 1I.
